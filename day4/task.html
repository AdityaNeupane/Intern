About us

Human service is the best way of life

Chitwan manav sewa ashram was established in 2072 B.S with a slogan "human service is the best way of life". We are located at Bharatpur , Chitwan. Chitwan manav sewa ashram is taking care of oldage and differently-abled people. Every person is equal and have equal right. So, we started this organiation for taking care of needy people.
